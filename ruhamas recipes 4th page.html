Measure Ingredients Accurately

Use a kitchen scale for precise measurements. This ensures consistent results and avoids common baking mistakes.

Room Temperature Ingredients

Using room temperature ingredients helps with even mixing and creates a better texture.
To quickly bring eggs to room temperature, place them in a bowl of warm water for a few minutes.

Don't Overmix

Overmixing your batter can lead to tough and dense baked goods. Mix just until the ingredients are combined.

Preheat Your Oven

Always preheat your oven before baking. An oven that isn't hot enough can cause uneven baking and poor texture.

Use Quality Ingredients

The quality of your ingredients can greatly impact the flavor and texture of your baked goods. Use the best ingredients you can afford for the best results.
